ENROLLMENT ROUTING PROCEDURE

1

Check applicable documentary requirements list.

2

Proceed to Vocational/Career Guidance Unit for aptitude/ability test and interest assessment.

3

Proceed to Clinic to undergo medical/health assessment.

4

Submit all checked documentary requirements to the Office of the Registrar.

5

Obtain an Order of Payment slip from the Registrar (for non-scholarship accounts).

6

Proceed to Cashier's Section to settle fees and receive Official Receipt.

7

Proceed to the TET Office for signature.

8

Return to the Registrar for T2MIS & BSRS entry and official "Officially Enrolled" stamping.

9

The Registrar shall issue a copy of the Enrollment Routing & Confirmation Slip to the trainee.
